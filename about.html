Hello and welcome to my porfolio!

My name is Katie Henry. I am a game design student at George Mason University specializing in game art. I've had a passion for playing games ever since I was young, but lately it's been overtaken by a desire to make them. Now I find myself taking apart games in my head while I play them. While I'm known at school as one of the best in my class, now I have to prove myself as a professional. I'm looking forward to that challenge and continuing to learn new things about the industry.
